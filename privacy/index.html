Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for the Sidefy macOS app (local-only data processing) and sidefyapp.com.

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This page covers two separate contexts: the Sidefy macOS application and the sidefyapp.com website. They are described in different sections below.

Sidefy Application

Sidefy (referred to as “we” or “us”) values your privacy. This section explains how the macOS app handles your information.

Data Collection

Local Processing: Sidefy processes all your data locally on your Mac. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to external servers through the app.

No Analytics: The Sidefy application does not use analytics tracking tools or services that collect information about your usage patterns.

No Advertising: Sidefy does not contain advertisements or advertising tracking technologies.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Calendar data or content
  • Task lists or notes
  • System information or device details
  • Usage analytics or behavior data
  • Location information

Third-Party Services

Sidefy may connect to third-party services (such as weather APIs, calendar services) only when explicitly configured by you. In such cases:

  • Data is transmitted directly between your device and the third-party service
  • We do not intercept, store, or access this data
  • Each third-party service has its own privacy policy

Data Security

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • No app data is stored on our servers
  • Plugin data is processed according to individual plugin privacy policies

Plugin Privacy

When using third-party plugins:

  • Each plugin may have its own privacy policy
  • Plugins operate independently of Sidefy’s core privacy protections
  • We recommend reviewing plugin privacy policies before installation

Website (sidefyapp.com)

This section applies to our marketing website only — not the Sidefy macOS application.

Analytics: We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregate website traffic and page views. These tools do not access your Sidefy app data, calendars, reminders, or plugin content.

No Account System: The website does not require an account and does not collect the personal data types listed in the app section above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/sidefy-team/sidefy

This privacy policy is effective as of the date stated above.
