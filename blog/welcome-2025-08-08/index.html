Welcome to Sidefy!

After 100+ beta iterations, Sidefy (v0.0.1) is finally here! This sidebar information hub brings together your calendar events, reminders, and RSS feeds in one elegant timeline.

Key features include system calendar integration, plugin architecture, and customizable themes. Thank you to all beta testers for making this possible.
